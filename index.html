About Me

I’m a front-end developer with about four years of experience building responsive websites and interactive experiences for a range of clients, from workplace safety to EV fleet management. I enjoy creating clean, scalable components and bringing them to life with thoughtful animations (often using GSAP), while making sure everything stays fast, accessible, and easy to use. A lot of my work has lived in HubSpot and WordPress, where I’ve built custom modules, landing pages, and themes, along with integrations that connect tools like HubSpot, GitHub, and Vercel to make sites more dynamic and data-driven.

Lately, I’ve been focused on growing into a full-stack developer. I’ve been spending time working with React, Node.js, and TypeScript, building out projects that go beyond the front end and into application logic and data flow. The goal is to move toward building complete, end-to-end experiences—not just polished interfaces, but the systems behind them too.
