About Me

I’m a front-end web developer with four years of experience building responsive websites and interactive experiences, with a focus on clean, scalable components and thoughtful animations using GSAP. I’ve worked extensively in HubSpot and WordPress, developing custom modules, landing pages, emails, and full themes tailored to business needs. My approach prioritizes performance and usability—optimizing page speed, designing mobile-first, and ensuring every build is fully responsive across devices. I also focus on creating modern, engaging experiences that not only look polished but function as effective conversion tools. Currently, I’m expanding into full-stack development with React, Node.js, and TypeScript to broaden the impact of my work.
